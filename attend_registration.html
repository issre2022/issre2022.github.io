Registration

 

Please read the information below:

 

The early registration closes on September 30, 2022. The Author registration deadline is September 23, 2022.

 

Authors, please note the following:

  1. For each paper to be included in the Proceedings, at least one author must register with full IEEE/CCF Member or IEEE/CCF non-member registration. This includes the authors of research papers, workshops, industry track, and Fast Abstracts. If the only author registering for the Symposium is a student, he/she cannot use student rates.
  2. Doctoral Symposium authors can register using student rates.
  3. All refund/cancellation requests must be received by September 30, 2022. There will be an administrative fee of 20% deducted from each refund. There will be no refund for registrations cancelled after September 30, 2022.
  4. Please note that the registration rate for all authors is the same regardless of on-site or online attendance.

To start your registration, please click on the following link: Online Registration Link (the link will open shortly).
