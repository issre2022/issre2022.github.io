Full Program

 

The ISSRE 2022 full program can be viewed below.
You may download the full program, which includes both the on-site program in Charlotte and the online program.

ISSRE is using Microsoft Teams as the online platform. The authors and all registered attendees were sent emails with the credentials and instructions on how to access the ISSRE 2022 online program, with a subject line “Credentials and Instructions to Access ISSRE 2022 Online Program”. (Please note that if you are logged in to Teams with another user account, you will need to logout and then login using your ISSRE 2022 credentials.)

If there is any issue with online access to the conference's platform please contact Jianwen Xiang.
