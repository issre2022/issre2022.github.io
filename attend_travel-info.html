Travel Information

 

Charlotte Douglas International Airport (CLT) is one of the world's busiest airports with 118,636 people traveling to, from and though CLT each day. CLT offers nonstop air service to 183 destinations around the globe and is home to eight major air carriers, 15 regional carriers and three foreign flag carriers.

For more flight and airport information, please click Charlotte Douglas International Airport.

The distance from the airport to the hotel is around 16 miles. Attendees could take a taxi (Airport is served by multiple taxi companies, UBER, Lyft and other companies) from Airport to the Hotel. Typical cost of one-way ride is ~$40 (for a price estimation, please click here).
