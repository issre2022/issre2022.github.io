Conference Venue

 

UNC Charlotte Marriott Hotel & Conference Center

 

The hotel is the gateway connecting the UNC Charlotte main campus, University Research and Innovation business parks and the city's business, shopping and entertainment districts. It is connected by light rail to the many attractions of downtown Charlotte, as well as the Charlotte Convention Center and The Dubois Center at UNC Charlotte Center City. NoDa, Charlotte Motor Speedway and PNC Music Pavilion are also within easy reach. Enjoy casual dining at Golden Owl Tavern, which showcases regional flavors, or sip a cold beverage in the lobby bar.
