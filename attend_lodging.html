The hotel is the gateway connecting the UNC Charlotte main campus, University Research and Innovation business parks and the city's business, shopping and entertainment districts. NoDa, Charlotte Motor Speedway and PNC Music Pavilion are also within easy reach. Enjoy casual dining at Golden Owl Tavern, which showcases regional flavors, or sip a cold beverage in the lobby bar.
